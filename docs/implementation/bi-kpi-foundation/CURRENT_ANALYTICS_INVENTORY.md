# Current Analytics Inventory

Existing reusable assets:

- Operational Heatmaps: reusable dashboard and signal summarization patterns.
- Operational Geography: reusable Organization/team/geography dimensions.
- Account Intelligence: existing account, SKU, deduction, and AI-ready account insight data.
- Supervisor Intelligence: existing alert and scheduled report concepts.
- Prediction Engine: deterministic forecast test pattern, currently experimental analytics.
- Route Replay: driver GPS trail, route event, export, and replay analytics.
- Route Manifest data: planned/actual route and stop fields, cases, deliveries, inventory, returns, documents, closeouts.

Existing but hardcoded or unversioned:

- Heatmap category weights.
- Supervisor alert/report logic.
- Route replay safety analytics.
- Delivery settlement and manifest totals.
- Prediction calculations.

Organization-specific:

- Drivers, routes, stops, manifests, delivery documents, delivery notes, route sessions, route events, operational reports, KPIs, KPI snapshots, dashboards, exports, and alerts.

Platform-global:

- Low-clearance bridge reference data and approved shared safety records.

Experimental:

- Prediction runs, AI operations status, and account intelligence AI insights.

Missing before this phase:

- Configurable KPI definitions.
- Formula versioning.
- Historical KPI reproducibility.
- Immutable KPI snapshots.
- KPI lineage and calculation trace.
- Organization-scoped dashboard model.
- KPI-specific alert rules and bounded exports.
