# Shared Safety Media Boundary

## Rule

Shared Safety may publish knowledge, not customer operations.

## Private Media

Delivery photos, route evidence, receipts, signatures, customer media, and private hazard submission attachments remain Organization-private unless separately reviewed and sanitized.

## Publication Boundary

Only explicitly approved sanitized media URLs may be attached to platform-global Shared Safety records.

## Validation

The Shared Safety service requires sanitized media and rejects private operational references before publication.
