# Glossary

## Organization

A customer company using the platform. The primary tenant boundary.

## Tenant

A logically isolated customer environment within the shared SaaS platform.

## Organization-private data

Data that belongs exclusively to one Organization.

## Shared Safety Intelligence

Approved truck-safety information visible across Organizations after platform review.

## KPI

Key Performance Indicator. A measurable metric that indicates performance.

## BI

Business Intelligence. Systems that convert data into reports, dashboards, trends, and decisions.

## RBAC

Role-Based Access Control.

## Claim

A fact about an authenticated identity, such as user ID, Organization ID, or role.

## ADR

Architecture Decision Record.
