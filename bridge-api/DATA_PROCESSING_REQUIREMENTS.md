# Customer Data Processing Requirements

The pilot contract or DPA must identify:

1. Controller/business and processor/service-provider roles.
2. Authorized data categories, users, purposes, and geographic regions.
3. Driver notice/consent responsibilities for GPS, photos, and signatures.
4. Retention periods, deletion deadlines, legal holds, and export format.
5. Approved subprocessors: Render, database host, Cloudflare R2 or configured
   object storage, Expo/EAS, Google Maps Platform, and OpenAI when enabled.
6. Encryption, access control, incident notification, audit, and support terms.
7. Cross-border transfer and data-residency requirements.
8. Customer instructions for account, route, receipt, and employee data.
9. End-of-pilot return/deletion certification.
10. Prohibition on model training or unrelated secondary use of customer data.

Engineering must not mark the DPA complete until counsel and the customer sign
the applicable agreement.
